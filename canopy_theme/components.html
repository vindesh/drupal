          Workforce Activity Report December 2020

          The latest UKG Workforce Activity Report revealed a 3.2% shift contraction in December a significant decline compared to the 0.1% growth in Novemberas the anticipated slowdown of the holiday season, coupled with COVID-19-related restrictions, brought the national labor recovery to a standstill. The UKG Workforce Recovery Scale, a measure of shift work losses and gains during the COVID-19 pandemic, currently sits at 84.3 out of 100its lowest level since July.
